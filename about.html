About D'vine

D'vine is a luxury Salon and Spa in Pune. We combine state of the art technology with top quality products to give you a pampering like never before. Our goal is to provide you services to help you achieve your best look and live your best life. Our well a equipped salon, sumptuous spa and highly trained staff are a sure way to help you relax and feel revitalized. We are also a franchise of Niks International, a world-renowned beauty and hair salon, and maintain the highest standards of service and quality.

About the owner

Tanvi Labhade

Tanvi Labhade, the owner of D'vine, has always been passionate about health and beauty and is a huge Spa aficionado. She understands the pressures that we all face to look our best and wants to make it a fun experience for her clients. She is also a firm believer that a relaxing session at a spa is the best way to unwind after a busy spell. The city of Pune has a limited number of options when it comes to great spas. This is why she decided to venture into this field and set up the best spa in Pune on her own. Her wish is that every client that walks in her door leaves feeling great about themselves and confident about putting their best foot forward. Her efforts have already gained recognition in the wellness industry and she was recently also featured in Femina magazine for her skills as a female entrepreneur and owner of a luxury spa and salon.
