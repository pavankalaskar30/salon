About D'vine

D'vine is a luxury Salon and Spa in Pune. We combine state of the art technology with top quality products to give you a pampering like never before. Our goal is to provide you services to help you achieve your best look and live your best life. Our well a equipped salon, sumptuous spa and highly trained staff are a sure way to help you relax and feel revitalized. We are also a franchise of Niks International, a world-renowned beauty and hair salon, and maintain the highest standards of service and quality.

Salon

Our unisex salon has a wide array of services to help you update your look in keeping with the latest trends and fashions. From hair styling and coloring to grooming, mani-pedis and much more, we'll help you transform your appearance in no time. Our expert beauticians and hair-stylists will also help you choose and customize your looks and hairstyles to best suit your needs.

Spa

In order to look great, it is also important for you to feel great. Our spa section is fully equipped with steams, baths, Jacuzzis and soothing fragrances and oils. Our plush salon services are sure to pamper your body and relax your mind, so that you leave our spa feeling fully rejuvenated.
